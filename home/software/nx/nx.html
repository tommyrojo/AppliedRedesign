NX

Develop Products Faster with NX

Siemens NX software is an integrated product design, engineering and manufacturing solution that helps you deliver better products faster and more efficiently.

NX is the industry's most integrated, flexible and efficient solution for product design, engineering and manufacturing, enabling smarter decisions in an integrated product development environment. Select your product focus:

Advanced conceptual design, 3D modeling and documentation
Complete part manufacturing solutions for tooling, machining and quality inspection
Multi-discipline simulation for structural, motion, thermal, flow and multi-physics

At Applied CAx, we are NX power-users and our engineers utilize the software every day. This allows us to provide our clients with an unparalleled level of technical support and guidance.
