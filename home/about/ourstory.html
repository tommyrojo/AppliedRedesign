Our Story

Applied CAx is a joint venture of two respected professional services companies. Our mission is to provide clients with the world's most advanced engineering software, training and support, leveraging the combined knowledge of our partner companies Sherpa Design and Predictive Engineering.

This combination of a mechanical design firm and FEA consultancy allows us to deliver the deepest technical capabilities of any Siemens reseller in the North America. Twenty degreed engineers each with unique skills in CAD, CAM, CAE and PLM are available to deliver high quality support and solutions.

Applied CAx was founded in 2008 and has quickly grown to become a gold-level Siemens PLM solution partner with clients across North America. Our customers cover a diverse range of industries from aerospace and energy to automotive, marine and high tech electronics manufacturers. Over the years, companies have come to rely upon our real-world knowledge for software recommendations, instructor-led training, technical support and, above all, peace of mind.
Sherpa Design is a fast growing mechanical engineering and design services firm founded by Pat Barrett in 2001. Specializing in 3-D modeling using popular CAD systems such as NX, Pro/Engineer and Solidworks, Sherpa employs more than a dozen top notch mechanical and design engineers. Sherpa's services include rapid prototyping, product design and on-site mechanical engineering contract services. In addition, Sherpa Design offers clients 3-D printing, coordinated measurement inspections (CMM) and short run machine shop (CNC) services.
Predictive Engineering is a finite element analysis (FEA) consultancy founded by George Laird, Ph.D., P.E. in 1995. Specializing in analysis tools Femap & NX Nastran, CFdesign and LS-DYNA, Predictive provides clients with a host of advanced engineering simulation and analysis services. Whether it be software solutions or consulting, Predictive Engineering has the knowledge, experience and technology to help your company take full advantage of FEA.
Applied CAx is located in beautiful Portland, Oregon.
